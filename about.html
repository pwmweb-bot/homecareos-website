About homecareOS

The story behind the platform built to transform home care management.

Our Story

Built From Real-World Experience

Philip Martin spent over 13 years in the home care software industry providing cloud-based solutions and helping users move time-consuming, manual tasks into streamlined digital workflows.

After spending years working for a PLC and developing his craft for software development, he saw first-hand the gaps in existing platforms. Agencies deserved more than fragmented tools stitched together with workarounds.

That insight led him to create homecareOS — a full holistic operating system for home care agencies. One platform that brings scheduling, care plans, compliance, communication, and growth tools under a single roof.

Our Mission

Why We Exist

To empower home care agencies across the UK with technology that simplifies operations, ensures compliance, and lets carers focus on what matters most — delivering exceptional care.

Designed for the People Who Care

We believe that when technology gets out of the way, carers can do what they do best. homecareOS removes the administrative burden so your team can spend more time with the people who need them.

  • Reduce admin time by up to 60%
  • Stay CQC-ready with built-in compliance tools
  • Empower your team with intuitive, modern software
