FAMILY & SERVICE USER PORTAL

Because Families Shouldn't Have to Wonder

Families deserve to know their loved one is being cared for — not just trust that it's happening. homecareOS gives every family member a secure portal with real-time visit notifications, care note summaries, carer profiles, and instant missed visit alerts. It's the transparency that builds trust, reduces anxious phone calls, and lets families feel connected to the care their loved one receives every single day.

Built for Families

What Families Actually Need

Not another app to learn. Just simple, reassuring access to the information that matters most.

Real-Time Visit Notifications

Families know the moment a carer arrives and leaves. No more anxious wondering whether today's visit happened.

Care Notes Access

Carer Profiles

Missed Visit Alerts

Full Feature List

Complete Family Transparency

Every feature designed to keep families informed, connected, and reassured about the care their loved one receives.

Real-time visit arrival/departure notifications

Push notifications the moment a carer checks in or out, so families always know when care is being delivered.

Visit summary and care notes access

After each visit, families can read a clear summary of what happened — tasks completed, mood observations, and any concerns flagged by the carer.

Carer profile with photo and qualifications

See the name, photo, qualifications, and experience of every carer assigned to their loved one — building trust before the doorbell rings.

Missed visit instant alerts

If a scheduled visit doesn't happen, the family is notified immediately — not hours later. No other platform offers this.

Medication administration visibility

Families can see which medications were administered during each visit, providing an extra layer of safety and accountability.

Care plan summary access

A clear, accessible summary of the current care plan so families understand what care is being delivered and why.

Secure messaging with agency

A dedicated, secure channel for families to ask questions, raise concerns, or share updates — without chasing the office by phone.

Visit feedback submission

Families and service users can rate visits and leave feedback, giving agencies direct insight into care quality.

Upcoming schedule visibility

See the full week ahead — who's coming, when, and for how long — so families can plan around visits with confidence.

Emergency contact information

Key emergency contacts and agency details always accessible, so families know exactly who to call in any situation.

Premium tier (£4.99/mo) with enhanced features

Unlock extended care history, detailed analytics, priority support, and richer care plan visibility for families who want the complete picture.

Service user portal (£3.99/mo) with direct access

Service users get their own portal to see who's coming, review their care plan, and provide feedback — putting them at the centre of their own care.
