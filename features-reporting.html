REPORTING & ANALYTICS

See Everything. Miss Nothing.

Real-time dashboards and automated reports replace hours of manual number-crunching. Whether you need a quick snapshot for your morning huddle or a detailed breakdown for your commissioner, homecareOS puts the right data in front of the right people at the right time — so you can act on insight, not instinct.

Actionable Intelligence

Reports That Drive Better Outcomes

Every report is designed to answer the questions that matter most to home care managers, coordinators, and commissioners.

Visit Reports

Completed, missed, cancelled, late arrivals. Filter by date, carer, service user, or area. Spot patterns before they become problems.

Compliance Reports

Training expiry, DBS status, supervision schedules, incident summaries. Everything your registered manager needs at a glance.

Staff Performance

Hours worked, visit punctuality, care note quality, absence patterns. Support your team with data, not assumptions.

Financial Reports

Revenue by payer, cost per visit, profit margins by service type. Understand your real profitability.

Service User Reports

Care plan reviews due, goal progress, observation trends. Evidence outcomes for commissioners and families.

Benchmarking (Pro)

Compare your agency's performance against sector averages. Identify where you're excelling and where you can improve.

Full Feature List

Comprehensive Reporting Suite

From real-time dashboards to commissioner-ready exports, every reporting tool has been built for the specific needs of UK home care.

8 built-in report types

Visit, compliance, staff performance, financial, service user, recruitment, incident, and benchmarking reports — all ready to run from day one.

Custom date range filtering

Slice any report by day, week, month, quarter, or custom date range to see exactly the time period you need.

Export to CSV and PDF

Download any report as a formatted PDF for sharing or as a CSV for further analysis in your own spreadsheets.

Scheduled automated reports

Set reports to generate and deliver automatically on a daily, weekly, or monthly schedule — straight to the right people's inboxes.

Real-time dashboard widgets

Configurable dashboard widgets show live KPIs the moment you log in — no waiting for last month's numbers.

KPI tracking and trending

Track key performance indicators over time and spot trends before they become issues — or opportunities you might miss.

Sector benchmarking (Pro tier)

Compare your agency against anonymised sector averages on key metrics like visit completion, staff retention, and training compliance.

Drill-down from summary to detail

Click any metric to drill down from the high-level overview to the individual visits, carers, or service users behind the numbers.

Report sharing and distribution

Share reports with colleagues, managers, or external stakeholders via email or secure link — with role-based access controls.

Commissioner-ready formatted exports

Generate reports in the exact format local authorities and NHS commissioners expect — no manual reformatting required.

Visual charts and graphs (Recharts)

Beautiful, interactive charts bring your data to life — bar charts, line graphs, pie charts and heat maps make patterns instantly visible.

Historical data comparison

Compare this week to last week, this month to last month, or any two periods side by side to measure progress and spot regressions.
