FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans and billing.

The Free plan gives you full access to core scheduling, digital care plans, the carer mobile app with GPS check-in/out, MAR records, basic compliance checklists, and basic reporting. It supports up to 5 staff, 25 service users, and 1 admin user. No credit card is required and there is no time limit — it is free forever.
On the Start and Pro plans, you pay a fixed amount per staff member per month. "Staff" means active carers and field workers on your roster. Admin and coordinator users are unlimited and do not count towards your staff total. If your team size changes, your bill adjusts automatically the following month.
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. When you upgrade, the new features become available immediately. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle. There are no penalties for switching.
No. All homecareOS plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Annual billing simply gives you a 20% discount — you can still cancel at any time with no fees. We believe our platform should earn your business every month.
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), processed securely through Stripe. Annual plans can also be paid by BACS bank transfer on request — contact us to arrange this.
When you reach 5 staff on the Free plan, you will be prompted to upgrade to Start or Pro. Your existing data is fully preserved — nothing is lost. You can continue using the platform with your current staff while you decide which plan to move to.
