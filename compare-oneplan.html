homecareOS vs OnePlan

OnePlan (now OneTouch Essentials) offers cloud-based care management with mobile apps and CQC compliance. homecareOS delivers AI-powered scheduling, Bradford Factor, and a free-forever model.

OnePlan

OnePlan (now rebranded as OneTouch Essentials) is a cloud-based domiciliary care platform from oneplansoftware.co.uk. It offers rostering with templates, finance modules including invoicing and payroll, a mobile app with NFC/QR check-in and eMAR, care planning with a form builder, and family and medical portals. It targets dom care, residential and assisted living providers — but lacks AI features, Bradford Factor scoring, four-nation compliance and transparent pricing.

Finds the best available carer in seconds, scoring on distance, continuity, hours headroom, preferences and schedule fit. OnePlan has no AI capabilities.

AI Rota Builder Pro

Builds a full week's rota automatically, optimising for carer preferences, travel time and continuity of care. OnePlan requires fully manual scheduling.

Bradford Factor Scoring

Automatic absence pattern analysis using the S² × D formula with traffic-light thresholds. No competitor in the home care space calculates this — including OnePlan.

Free Forever

homecareOS core platform is permanently free for every agency. OnePlan charges for its software but does not publicly disclose pricing.

Four-Nation Compliance

Native support for England (CQC), Wales (CIW), Scotland (Care Inspectorate) and Northern Ireland (RQIA). OnePlan focuses on CQC compliance only.

homecareOS: Yes / OnePlan: No

Family Missed Visit Alerts

Instant notification to family members when a visit is missed, providing peace of mind and transparency. OnePlan does not offer this feature.
