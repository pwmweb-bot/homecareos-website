HOW IT WORKS

From Sign-Up to Go Live in 9 Steps

No complicated setup. No consultant fees. Just follow the steps and you're running.

Sign Up for Free

Create your account in under 2 minutes. Free for agencies with fewer than 5 staff — no credit card, no commitment. Larger teams get a full-feature trial.

Import Your Data

We help you import data from your existing provider — even if they are slow to help, or not helpful at all. Simply upload whatever you can export, print or access and we'll get it into the system for you.

Create Carers & Service Users

Add your team and the people they care for. Profiles, contact details, care needs, medications, risk assessments — everything in one place from day one.

Create Visits

Build your rota with drag-and-drop scheduling. Set recurring patterns, assign carers, and let the system handle travel time and conflict detection.

Invite Your Team

Send invites to your carers, family members, and the rest of the office team. Everyone gets their own purpose-built portal — no training needed.

Push the Big GO LIVE Button!

When you're ready, hit Go Live. Your agency is now running on homecareOS. Your dashboard lights up with real-time data from the very first visit.

Carers Hit the Road

Your carers see their visits on the mobile app and go about their normal day. Any support they need is right there on their phone — or they can contact homecareOS support via text, email, message, call, or our AI assistant.

Stay on Top of Alerts

Real-time alerts keep your office informed the moment something needs attention. Late visits, missed check-ins, schedule changes — react with confidence, not guesswork.

Invoices, Payroll & Compliance

Generate invoices, run payroll, and produce CQC compliance reports with a few clicks. Everything is already captured from the visits — no double entry, no spreadsheets.

SEE IT IN ACTION

Built for the Way Home Care Really Works

Scroll to explore the platform — every screen designed for care, not generic project management.

Dashboard

Your agency at a glance — today's visits, completion rates, active carers, late visit alerts, and compliance status. Everything a manager needs to start the day with confidence.

Drag-and-Drop Rota

The visual scheduling grid that coordinators love. See every carer, every visit, and every travel gap on one screen. Move visits in seconds with full conflict detection.

Live Visit Flow Map

Watch your carers move through the day on a real-time map. Spot late arrivals, understand travel patterns, and see visit status at a glance — with a timeline you can scrub through.

Staff Management

Every carer's details, role, contact info, DBS expiry, and contracted hours in one searchable list. Filter by employment status or job role. Add new carers in seconds.

CQC Compliance Hub

Training records, compliance framework self-assessment, PIR export, CQC notifications, complaints, safeguarding, audits, incidents, and improvement plans — all in one place, always audit-ready.
