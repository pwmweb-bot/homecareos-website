Staff Management

Your Carers Are Your Greatest Asset. Treat Them That Way.

Retaining good carers is the single biggest challenge in home care. High turnover drives up costs, disrupts continuity of care, and puts your CQC rating at risk. homecareOS gives you the tools to track training, monitor absence patterns, manage leave, schedule supervisions and identify retention risks before your best people walk out the door.

Tools That Help You Retain and Develop Your Team

📚

Training Tracking

Monitor mandatory and specialist training across your entire team. Automatic alerts 30, 14, and 7 days before expiry.

🛡

DBS & Registration Monitoring

Track DBS checks, professional registrations (SCW, SSSC, NISCC) with renewal dates and document storage.

📊

Bradford Factor Scoring

Automatic absence pattern analysis. S² × D calculation with RAG thresholds (0–99 green, 100–199 amber, 200+ red). No competitor offers this.

🏖

Leave Management

Holiday requests, approval workflows, accrual tracking, and calendar integration. Carers can request leave directly from the mobile app.

📋

Supervision Scheduling

Plan and record 1-to-1 supervisions, annual appraisals, and competency assessments. Never miss a scheduled review.

🤖

Retention Risk Scoring

AI-powered analysis identifies carers at risk of leaving based on absence patterns, declined shifts, and engagement metrics.
