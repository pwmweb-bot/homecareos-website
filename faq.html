Your data belongs to you, always. Under UK GDPR regulations, you have the legal right to request a full copy of all your data from any provider. We can help you navigate this process and have extensive experience working with other providers to ensure a smooth data transfer. If needed, we can assist you with formal data portability requests under Article 20 of GDPR. We have never encountered a situation where we couldn't successfully retrieve a client's data.
